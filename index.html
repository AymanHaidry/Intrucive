Common questions

Is Intrucive really free?
Yes. The full learning program is free for individual learners. Company dashboard features for teams have a separate pricing model.
How long does it take to complete?
It is entirely self-paced. Most learners complete the core program in 4–8 weeks working an hour or two per day. You can go faster or slower as needed.
Do I need any technical background?
None at all. The program is designed for office employees, managers, HR professionals, and anyone who wants to work faster with AI. No coding required.
How are certificates verified?
Every certificate has a unique ID and a QR code that links to a public verification page. Anyone can confirm the certificate is genuine in seconds.
How does the company account work?
Employees sign up with your company's admin key, which connects them to your organization. The company admin dashboard then shows team-wide progress, certificate status, and skill coverage.
